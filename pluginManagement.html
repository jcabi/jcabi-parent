Pre-Defined Maven Plugins

com.jcabi:parent POM declares a number of most useful plugins in its pluginManagement section. You can use them without specifying version. Thus, you don't need to worry about keeping versions up to date. When you're ready to upgrade just change the version of our parent POM and all plugins will be upgraded automatically.

All important maven-*-plugin plugins are there.

Check out pom.xml for a complete list of plugins.
